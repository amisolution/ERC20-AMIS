F. A. Q.
1. How SAFE is it?
The smart contract algorithm on which the project is based is uploaded to the blockchain Ethereum
with the OWNERSHIP WAIVER function. This means that nobody - including the organizers-
can access it to tamper with its independent functioning.

However, DO NOT INVEST BORROWED MONEY OR MONEY YOU CANNOT AFFORD TO LOSE in the project.
2. Why OUR project?
We have analyzed ALL the current, pre-existing and announced similar projects
with their advantages and vulnerabilities. Using this information, we has optimized
and SECURED financial model by setting the distribution 90% - 5% - 3% - 2% - 1%.
Most importantly, only OUR smart-the contract was awarded to FULL automation, and passed
the test of vulnerability.
3. Why do we call dividend payments LIFE-LONG
It is written in a smart contract, no one and nothing can prevent it, because
it activated the "REFUSAL of OWNERSHIP". The payments will continue,
while in the Fund to payoff funds.
4. What will happen to the project if the website stops working?
The website carries more information function, if for any reason, he will cease
to work, it will not affect the DAILY interest payments and receiving deposits.
5. What will happen to the project if its ORGANIZERS disappear?
The project is INITIALLY completely self-contained and lives on the network, the developers
will not be able to affect him.
6. Is there any referral rewards system?
Yes there is, it works as follows:
At the moment of making the deposit, the referral indicates in the DATA field the ETH address of the referrer's wallet,
and the referrer then receives 3% of the first attachment of the referral, and the contribution of the referral
the system counts with a coefficient of + 3.33% (put 100, the system fixes as 103.33).
Referral payments are made by one transaction with daily payments by your request.
