    What is Amis Forum live?

    AMIS Forum is a live event to exhibit AMIS DELTA DEX, a new generation of decentralized exchange where blockchain based peer-peer can take place. The trading platform will be dedicated for the agricultural and grain market around the globe. Our goal is to provide a secure and transparent decentralized exchange platform almost free from any transaction or processing fees.
    We are decentralizing the agricultural and grain market industry with Blockchain and Secure Identity Platform

    How does AMIS DELTA DEX make money?

    AMIS DELTA DEX will be a sustainable subscriber-based model. We will not make any money from the transactions but we will only inject cryptoderivatives instruments pegged at 1:1 with AMIS token reserve to run the rewards and revenue program.

    How does AMIS DELTA DEX differ from other Agricultural Market Information System websites?

    We focus on free trade, contactless payments with almost no transaction fees or processing fees.
    Our main goal is to create very own infrastructure based on blockchain technology that will remove third parties and negate any transaction/processing fees for any subscribers globally.
    We are focused on working with existing community which now consists of millions of people and an audience that is not able to get free trade due to inflated prices and centralized market governance. We have created a system which will allow various generations of people to connect with crypto currencies and use them as a solid payment method for agricultural commodity trading in the grain market niche.

    How about other financial services? Do you plan to work with entities who still want to trade with FIAT currencies ?

    We do plan to bridge AMIS DELTA DEX platform with FIAT, BTC and certain designated altcoins.
