News & Press Release

AMIS Tokens – Powering The AMIS DELTA DEX PLATFORM

AMIS DELTA DEX is a platform for the people, by the people and of the people. The platform will be powered by AMI Tokens. The ERC20 compatible token will be the only Utility tokens that will be used on the platform for sale and purchase of tickets in the secondary re-sale market. The tokenization model focuses on real time conversion of ticket price into AMIS Tokens (value from exchange) and usage by both buyers and sellers.

The AMIS token will be listed on exchanges (to be announced) and will be transferrable to other cryptocurrencies and fiat currencies.

The AMIS TOKEN will be the only means of transaction on the AMIS DELTA DEX platform
